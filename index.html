Mr. Juicy Review

First off let me say that this is the first time in a while that I've gone out to eat ever since the whole covid situation kicked in and oh my... this was definitely worth going out for! If you've been craving a juicy burger lately with just the right amount of meat, cheese, and toppings, then look no further! Mr. Juicy is just the place for you! The fries are also on point! Crispy just as you'd expect fries to taste! (because who likes soggy fries lol) I will definitely stop by again sometime and try out their milkshakes, which I hear are pretty popular as well! I'd definitely recommend dining in since these burgers are pretty "Juicy" and no one likes their meat in soggy buns once they make the trip back home (or simply eat in the car!)
